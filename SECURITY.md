# Security Policy

## Reporting Security Issues

The CZI team takes security bugs seriously. We appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose your findings, and will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.

To report a security issue, please email security@chanzuckerberg.com with a description of the issue, the steps you took to create the issue, affected versions, and, if known, mitigations for the issue.

Our security team will respond within 3 working days of your email. If the issue is confirmed as a vulnerability, we will open a Security Advisory and acknowledge your contributions as part of it. This project follows a 90 day disclosure timeline.
